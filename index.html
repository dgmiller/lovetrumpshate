I'm Derek Miller

I turn business problems into math problems, then I solve them with code and design. I'm an scientist by day, designer by night. I have experience with data visualization, creative writing, and museum exhibit design. Well-designed objects and spaces make the world a better place. My background is in math, stats, and economics. Currently working as a course developer and instructor for a class on analytical visualization and exploratory data analysis, a Junior Data Scientist for Meter Group, and a student in Applied and Computational Mathematics.
